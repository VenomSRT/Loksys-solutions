Freight Owners

For Companies that move their goods in Shipping Containers who want to reduce the Risk when moving goods we have the answer.

When moving goods by Container it is important to ensure that your goods receive the optimum protection whilst in Transit, that you know where your goods are and the condition of these goods and that your Transport partners are conforming to the Service Level Agreement.

Trakalok provides all the technology and services to support this. The unique combination of Hardware and Software will enable goods owners to add real benefit to their Supply Chain Risk Management activities.

Large Logistics Companies

As supply chains become increasingly complex it is important the Logistics Providers offer the optimum solutions.
